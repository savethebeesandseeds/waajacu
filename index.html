Method

A clear operating model before code, claims, or commitments.

The work is organized so clients can evaluate progress without depending on vague promises or hidden context.

01

Establish source of truth.

Define the official channels, project goals, ownership, and constraints before delivery begins.

02

Map the risks.

Identify security, quality, dependency, data, and maintenance risks in language that can be acted on.

03

Build in verifiable units.

Ship work in small increments with reviewable decisions, implementation notes, and explicit acceptance criteria.

04

Leave useful artifacts.

Deliver documentation, source references, and next-step plans that remain useful after the engagement ends.
